Plastic vs Steel Moulds: 5 Key Factors

📅 May 30, 2026🏷️ Material Comparison⏱️ 6 min read

Plastic moulds offer significant advantages over steel for many applications. We break down the five critical factors.

1. Durability

Plastic: 300+ cycles with proper material quality. No rust or corrosion.

Steel: Can last 1000+ cycles but requires regular maintenance to prevent rust.

2. Cost

Plastic: 60-70% cheaper upfront. No maintenance costs.

Steel: Higher initial cost. Regular maintenance and coating renewal required.

3. Maintenance

Plastic: Minimal. Clean with mild detergent. No special storage needed.

Steel: Requires rust protection, oiling, and controlled storage environment.

4. Production Efficiency

Plastic: Lightweight, easier to handle. Faster cooling cycles.

Steel: Heavy, requires lifting equipment. Slower demoulding.

5. ROI Comparison

For most precast applications, plastic moulds deliver higher ROI due to lower total cost of ownership and simpler operations.
