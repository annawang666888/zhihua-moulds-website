D400 vs F900 Manhole Cover Mould Comparison

📅 May 30, 2026 🏷️ Product Comparison ⏱️ 6 min read

Understanding EN 124 load classes is crucial for municipal projects. This detailed comparison breaks down when to use D400 versus F900 moulds.

Load Class Overview

Load Class Max Load Application Typical Size
D400 40 tonnes Municipal, highways, pedestrian areas Ø 600-800 mm
F900 90 tonnes Airports, heavy industrial, high-speed rail Ø 800-900 mm

Key Differences

  • Concrete Volume: F900 requires 20-30% more concrete per cast
  • Mould Weight: F900 moulds are 25-35% heavier
  • Cycle Life: Both should achieve 300+ cycles with proper material
  • Cost: F900 moulds cost 30-40% more due to material and design complexity

Selection Criteria

Choose D400 if:

  • Urban municipal infrastructure
  • Residential and light commercial areas
  • Standard highway applications
  • Budget constraints are a consideration

Choose F900 if:

  • Airport runways and taxiways
  • Industrial facilities with heavy forklift traffic
  • High-speed railway platforms
  • Projects requiring maximum load capacity
