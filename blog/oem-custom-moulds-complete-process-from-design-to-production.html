OEM Custom Moulds: Complete Process from Design to Production

📅 May 30, 2026🏷️ Process⏱️ 6 min read

From your initial drawing to the finished prototype and production mould, follow the complete OEM workflow.

Step 1: Drawing Review

Send us your 2D CAD drawings or PDFs. Our engineering team reviews for manufacturability and provides feedback within 24 hours.

Step 2: Quotation

Receive a detailed quote including material cost, tooling fees, and lead time. Standard SKU modifications are free; new cavities quoted based on complexity.

Step 3: Prototype

We produce a sample mould for your approval. Cast a test piece, verify dimensions, and confirm quality before full production.

Step 4: Production

After prototype approval, we move to full production. Typical turnaround: 7-15 days for standard projects, 20-30 days for complex custom designs.
