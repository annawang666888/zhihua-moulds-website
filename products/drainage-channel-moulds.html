What to send for a fast quote

For drainage products, a drawing or clear sample photo helps us confirm demoulding direction, wall thickness, reinforcement ribs and whether a one-piece or multi-part mould is better.

  • Finished concrete product length, width, height and wall thickness
  • Slot, hole or cover pattern requirements
  • Expected material: PP, ABS or custom recommendation
  • Quantity, destination country and preferred container loading plan
  • Whether logo, lettering or local-standard markings are needed

Drainage Mould FAQ

Can you make drainage moulds from drawings or samples?

Yes. We can review drawings, dimensions or sample photos and suggest a mould structure for production and demoulding.

Can several drainage products be shipped together?

Yes. Mixed-SKU container orders are common for drainage projects, especially when customers need U-drains, covers and ditch slabs together.

Do you only sell the six products shown here?

No. These six images are representative examples for the website. Zhihua has more catalogue styles and can also make OEM drainage moulds.
