Paver Block Mould FAQ

Can these moulds produce coloured pavers?

Yes. These moulds can be used with pigment-coloured concrete for common red, yellow, grey, black and decorative paving products.

Can I order several paving patterns together?

Yes. Mixed-SKU orders are common for paver and landscaping products, especially when customers test multiple local-market patterns.

Can you copy a local paving stone pattern?

We can review a sample photo, dimensions or drawing and suggest whether a practical plastic mould structure can be made.
