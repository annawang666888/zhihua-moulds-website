What to send for a fast quote

Manhole Cover Mould FAQ

Can you make manhole cover moulds from drawings or samples?

Yes. Send the finished cover dimensions, drawing, markings or sample photos. We can review the mould structure and quote standard or custom production.

Can one shipment include round and square cover moulds?

Yes. Mixed-SKU orders are common for distributors and municipal supply projects where multiple cover sizes are needed.

Can the mould include logo or utility text?

Yes. Logo, lettering, utility name and anti-slip surface patterns can be discussed from drawings or clear sample photos.
