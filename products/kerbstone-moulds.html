What to send for a fast quote

Kerbstone projects often follow local road authority profiles. A cross-section drawing or clear product photo helps us confirm slope, radius, chamfer and demoulding direction.

  • Finished kerb length, width, height and profile drawing
  • Whether it is L-type, S-type, slope, straight, radius or corner type
  • Required radius or turning angle for curved sections
  • Application: road edge, median, sidewalk, parking or landscaping
  • Quantity, destination country and mixed-profile requirements

Kerbstone Mould FAQ

Can you make kerbstone moulds from local drawings?

Yes. Send a cross-section drawing, dimensions or sample photo. We can review the profile and suggest a mould structure.

Can I order straight and radius kerbs together?

Yes. Mixed-SKU orders are common for road projects where straight kerbs, curved kerbs and corner pieces are needed together.

Are these moulds only for road projects?

No. Kerbstone moulds are also used for landscaping edges, parks, residential communities, parking lots and public spaces.
