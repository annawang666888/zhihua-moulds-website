Applications & Industries

Our moulds produce the concrete elements that build and maintain public infrastructure worldwide.

🏙️ Municipal Works

City infrastructure relies on standardised precast concrete elements — manhole covers, drainage grates, kerbstones, and tree grates. Our moulds help municipal casting yards produce these components at scale, with consistent dimensions and surface quality.

We supply moulds that conform to common international specifications (EN 124, BS, GOST) and can customise cavity dimensions to match local standards.

  • Manhole covers for sewer and utility access
  • Kerbstones for road and pavement edges
  • Drainage channel covers for stormwater management
  • Tree grates for urban streetscapes
  • Planters for public space beautification
Related Products: Manhole Cover Moulds • Kerbstone Moulds • Drainage Cover Moulds • Tree Grate Moulds • Planter Moulds

🛣️ Highway Construction

Highway projects consume large volumes of kerbstones, median barriers, drainage covers, and slope protection blocks. Zhihua's moulds are designed for high-cycle production — each mould withstands 300+ pours with minimal dimensional change.

  • L-type and slope-type kerbstone moulds for road edging
  • Guardrail post moulds for median barriers
  • Drainage cover moulds for roadside gutters
  • Slope protection moulds for embankments and cut slopes
  • Cable trough moulds for roadside utility corridors
Related Products: Kerbstone Moulds • Slope Protection Moulds • Drainage Cover Moulds • Guardrail Post Moulds • Cable Trough Moulds

🚄 Railway & High-Speed Rail

Railway subgrade construction demands precision: cable troughs, slope protection, drainage, and platform kerbstones must meet tight tolerances. Our moulds are engineered for the repeated casting cycles required by rail projects, where thousands of identical units are needed on schedule.

  • Cable trough moulds for trackside power and signalling ducts
  • Slope protection moulds for rail embankments
  • Platform kerbstone moulds for passenger stations
  • Drainage cover moulds for ballast drainage
  • Guardrail moulds for railway boundary fencing
Related Products: Cable Trough Moulds • Slope Protection Moulds • Platform Kerbstone Moulds • Drainage Cover Moulds • Guardrail Post Moulds

🌊 Hydraulic Engineering

Canals, dams, riverbanks, and flood-control channels rely on slope protection blocks and drainage elements to resist erosion and manage water flow. Our hexagonal and arch-frame slope moulds produce the interlocking blocks used in large hydraulic projects across the Middle East and Central Asia.

  • Hexagonal slope blocks for canal and riverbank lining
  • Arch-frame moulds for dam spillway protection
  • Grid-frame moulds for landslide-prone slopes
  • Drainage cover moulds for irrigation channels
Related Products: Slope Protection Moulds • Drainage Cover Moulds

🌳 Urban Landscaping

Public plazas, pedestrian zones, parks, and commercial developments need decorative yet functional concrete elements — pavers, tree grates, planters, and feature blocks. Our moulds produce clean, consistent finishes that architects and landscape designers specify.

  • Interlocking paver moulds for pedestrian areas
  • Tree grate moulds for streetscape planting
  • Concrete planter moulds for public and private spaces
  • Decorative flagstone moulds for plazas and promenades
Related Products: Paver Moulds • Tree Grate Moulds • Planter Moulds • Flagstone Moulds

⚡ Utilities & Telecom

Power distribution, telecommunications, and water-supply networks need cable troughs, manhole access chambers, and utility covers. Our moulds produce the precast elements that protect underground infrastructure in urban and rural settings.

  • Cable trough moulds for power and fibre-optic ducts
  • Manhole cover moulds for utility access chambers
  • Drainage cover moulds for substation and switchyard areas
  • Custom moulds for non-standard utility enclosures
Related Products: Cable Trough Moulds • Manhole Cover Moulds • Drainage Cover Moulds • Custom Design Service
